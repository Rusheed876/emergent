## 2. Event Boost Products

### Pay-Per-Use Promotional Products

| Product | Price | Duration | Reach |
|---------|-------|----------|-------|
| **Featured Event** | $25 - $50 | 24 hours | Top of city feed |
| **Weekend Spotlight** | $150 - $200 | Fri-Sun | Highlighted section |
| **City Takeover** | $500 - $1,000 | 24 hours | Banner + push notification |
| **Genre Takeover** | $300 | 1 week | Top of genre filter |

### Product Details

#### Featured Event ($25-50)
- Event pinned to top of city feed for 24 hours
- "Featured" badge displayed
- 3-5x more visibility than organic listings
- Price varies by city (Kingston $25, Miami/NYC $50)

#### Weekend Spotlight ($150-200)
- Premium placement Friday through Sunday
- Highlighted in "This Weekend" filter
- Included in weekend push notification roundup
- Social media cross-promotion

#### City Takeover ($500-1,000)
- Full banner ad at top of city feed
- Push notification to all city users
- Featured in chat room announcement
- Social media promotion
- Best for major events (concerts, festivals)

#### Genre Takeover ($300/week)
- Top placement when users filter by your genre
- Great for niche events (Afrobeat nights, Reggae sessions)
- Exclusive - only one per genre per week
